    About Me

    I am a political science Ph.D. student at Penn State. My primary areas of focus are methodology and international relations. My substantive research generally deals with developing models that forecast international events of interest, such as interstate conflict. On the methodological side, I focus on using machine learning techniques in conjunction with big data sources like Open Event Data Alliance.
